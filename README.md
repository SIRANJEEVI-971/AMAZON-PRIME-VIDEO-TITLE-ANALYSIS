# 🎬 Amazon Prime Video Titles Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive **Amazon Prime Video Titles Analytics Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI**.

The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into Amazon Prime Video's content library by analyzing movies and TV shows based on genres, ratings, release years, countries, directors, and content types. It transforms raw entertainment data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI reporting.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze the total content available on Amazon Prime Video.
- Understand content distribution by genre.
- Analyze ratings across all titles.
- Compare Movies and TV Shows.
- Identify content availability by country.
- Analyze content growth over time.
- Support business decisions using interactive dashboards.

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains Amazon Prime Video content information including:

- Title
- Genre
- Director
- Country
- Rating
- Release Year
- Date Added
- Duration
- Content Type (Movie / TV Show)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Amazon Prime Video contains more than **9,600 titles**.
- Movies account for approximately **80%** of the content library.
- Drama is the most popular genre.
- Content has grown rapidly after 2015.
- The platform hosts content from multiple countries.
- A wide variety of ratings makes the platform suitable for different age groups.
